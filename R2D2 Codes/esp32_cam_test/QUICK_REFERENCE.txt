🐛 COMMON ISSUES
================================================================================

❌ Photos not saving
   → Check Serial Monitor for SPIFFS errors
   → May need to reformat SPIFFS

❌ Motion always triggering
   → Increase motionThreshold to 20-25%

❌ Not detecting motion
   → Decrease motionThreshold to 10-12%

❌ Can't connect WiFi
   → Wait 10 seconds after power on
   → Check power supply (5V 2A)

❌ Stream is laggy
   → Reduce frame rate
   → Lower JPEG quality
   → Check WiFi signal
